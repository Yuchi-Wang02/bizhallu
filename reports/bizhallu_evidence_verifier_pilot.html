Guardrail

This verifier pilot is not a production checker and not a new benchmark result. It does not claim superiority over internal detectors. It creates reviewable rows for the next comparison between internal uncertainty and evidence-aware verification.

Pilot interpretation

What this adds to the current detector story.

  • Internal uncertainty asks whether the model appeared uncertain while generating a token span.
  • Evidence-aware verification asks whether the generated business claim is supported by the evidence or gold answer.
  • Confident wrong evidence binding is the key failure mode: a span can reuse real values but attach them to the wrong product, rank, or conclusion.
  • The current pilot marks 7 contradicted spans where all three displayed internal detectors missed the error.
