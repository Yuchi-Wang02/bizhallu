Presentation guardrails

The strongest public version is honest about scope.

  • Say the project evaluates span-level business facts, not full report truthfulness.
  • Say labels are locked for presentation with assistant_full_review; do not claim a large human-labeled benchmark.
  • Say the data source is UCI Online Retail and the model run is local Qwen/Qwen3-0.6B.
  • Use q_0064 and q_0069 as the main case studies because they show realistic business-risk errors.
  • Frame the project as business analytics and AI reliability, not as a generic sales dashboard.
